### 1. Synthetic Market Stress Testing

* **Generative Scenario Engines:** Beyond standard Monte Carlo simulations, Ulysses uses generative models to create **Synthetic News Feeds** and "Black Swan" narratives (e.g., a simulated 2008 or 2020 crash) tailored to your specific portfolio to test your psychological breaking point.
* **AI FOMO Interceptor:** An anomaly detection model monitors trade velocity against market volatility. If it detects "chasing" behavior, it generates a personalized interventionâ€”showing you a generative projection of the "Emotional Cost" of your trade.

### 2. The Socratic Journal (Journal 2.0)

* **NLP Sentiment Parsing:** Using powerful local models, Ulysses parses your trade notes offline to identify triggers like fear, greed, or uncertainty.
* **Generative Debunking:** When the system detects a bias (e.g., "This time is different"), a Generative AI Coach engages you in **Socratic questioning**, forcing you to rationalize the trade against your long-term goals before the "Buy" button is unlocked.

* **Smart Pre-Commitment:** Define "If-Then" rules during calm periods. During high-stress volatility, the AI enforces these logic-based contracts, physically locking emotional overrides in the UI.
